# Frequently asked questions
**Where are my visits saved?**

Your visits are only saved on your device and are not stored online. Nobody else can access your visits.


**Can I edit a visit?**

Yes! You can do this by tapping on the visit on the overview, and then tapping on the pen in the top right corner. Tap the checkmark to save your changes, or on the arrow to discard your changes.


**Can I remove a visit?**

Yes! You can do this by swiping the visit to the left on the overview. Or by clicking on the visit and editing it, there you will see a button to remove the visit.


**How can I easily search for a specific visit / venue / performer?**

You can search by visit, venue or performer in the 'Search' tab.


**How can I transfer my visits to another device?**

At this moment it is not possible to easily transfer your visits to another device. You will have to enter your visits manually on another device. In the future, the app might add a login system which allows you to access  and edit your visits on multiple devices.


**What happens with my visits when I delete the app?**

When you delete the app all your visits are deleted as well. When you reinstall the app you will have to manually add your visits again.


**How can I change my privacy settings?**

In the menu is an option to change your privacy settings.


**I found a bug in the app or I have an idea for a new functionality. Where can I report these?**

You can email these to dev.esmee.kluijtmans@gmail.com.
